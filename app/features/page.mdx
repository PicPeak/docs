# Features

PicPeak includes everything a photographer needs to share event galleries with clients securely and professionally.

## Core capabilities

- **[Gallery Management](/features/gallery-management)** --- create events with categories, passwords, expiration dates, and bulk uploads.
- **[Draft Mode](/features/draft-mode)** --- prepare events before publishing them to clients.
- **[Themes & Branding](/features/themes-branding)** --- 11 built-in presets, custom CSS, white-label admin and login screens, and a public landing page.
- **[Email Notifications](/features/email-notifications)** --- multilingual templates for gallery creation, expiration warnings, and password delivery.
- **[Photo Protection](/features/photo-protection)** --- watermarking, right-click prevention, canvas rendering, and DevTools detection.
- **[External Media](/features/external-media)** --- reference an existing folder library without copying originals.
- **[Analytics](/features/analytics)** --- built-in dashboard for views, downloads, and engagement, plus optional Umami integration.
- **[Video Support](/features/video-support)** --- upload and stream MP4, WebM, MOV, and AVI alongside photos.
- **[Feedback](/features/feedback)** --- likes, ratings, comments, and admin moderation.
- **[Multi-Admin](/features/multi-admin)** --- multiple administrator accounts with role-based permissions.
